History of Italian Cars

Italy is known worldwide for producing some of the most beautiful and high perfomance cars ever made. The Italian automative industry began developing in the early 20th century with companies focusinng on speed, design, and passion, creating vehicles that are both powerful and visually stunning. Italy quickly became a centre for sports cars and luxury vehicles
